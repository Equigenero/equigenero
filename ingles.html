Welcome to Equigenero!

In a world where technology and innovation are at the forefront, gender equality in engineering is essential. Although we have made progress, women are still underrepresented in this field, which limits the variety of ideas that can emerge. This page aims to raise awareness about the importance of more women becoming interested in engineering. We want to share initiatives, resources and experiences that motivate women to pursue careers in this field. We believe that inclusion is key to a better future, where everyone, regardless of gender, has the same opportunities to contribute.
